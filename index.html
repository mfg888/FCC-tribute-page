Diana

The People's Princess

Princess Diana influenced millions of people from all over the world, and her legacy will remain forever. She was the mother of Prince William, currently in line for the throne after his father, Diana's former husband, and of Prince Harry. Diana was also known for her charity work and her fashion image.

Diana's Timeline
